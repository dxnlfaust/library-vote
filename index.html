How preferential voting works: AI Slop runneth:

When you vote, you rank the options in order of preference: 1 for your favourite, 2 for your second choice, and so on. You don't have to rank all options.

Counting works in rounds. In the first round, only everyone's first preference is counted. If one option has more than [threshold]% of the votes, it wins.

If no option reaches the threshold, the option with the fewest votes is eliminated. The people who voted for that option have their vote transferred to their next preference. This repeats until one option wins.

This means your vote is never "wasted" — if your favourite is knocked out, your support moves to your next choice automatically.
